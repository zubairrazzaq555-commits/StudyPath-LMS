Bilkul! First Law of Thermodynamics asal mein Law of Conservation of Energy hai.

Iska matlab ye hai ke energy na create ki ja sakti hai na destroy, bas ek form se doosri form mein change hoti hai.

ΔQ = ΔU + ΔW

Jitni heat (ΔQ) aap system ko dein ge, wo do cheezon mein divide hogi: uski internal energy (ΔU) barhanay mein aur kuch kaam (ΔW) karnay mein.

Ye concept thermodynamics ki foundation hai. Kya ye baat clear hai ya koi confusion hai?
